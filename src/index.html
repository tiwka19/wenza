What We Produce

  • Carbide Tools

    The most common cutting tool material in modern plants. The application of carbide inserts depends on the combination of the carbide insert material and the type of coating (or lack thereof).

    The great advantage of this tool is that it does not react with the material to be machined.

  • Polycrystalline Diamond (PCD) Inserts

    Used for high speed machining of all types of aluminium, copper, non-ferrous materials, glass, ceramics, graphite, carbide, high hardness carbides and glass filled polymers.

    High-speed machining with PCD tools can reach speeds of up to 4,000 m/min and offers greater efficiency and precision, as well as a multiplication of the production output.

  • Cubic Boron Nitride (CBN) Inserts

    Tools made of super-hard material that approaches the hardness, strength and wear resistance properties of diamond.

    The main application of these tools is the machining of high hardness materials (45...67 HRC) as well as cast irons at high speeds.

  • Ceramics Inserts

    Ceramics inserts enable the machining of hard materials under unfavourable conditions, significantly increase the machining efficiency of modern hard-to-machine cast irons and bring a new level of productivity to the machining of heat-resistant alloys.

    This tool material is based on aluminium oxide, silicon nitride (or carbide), has a higher hardness and red-hardness than carbide tools but a comparatively lower strength.

  • Solid Round Tools

    End mills and carbide drills provide good productivity, tool life and high surface quality.

    Solid cutting tools are more stable and precise than equivalent indexable tools. Also, this type of tool does not impose stringent requirements on the tool stability and overhang in conventional machining.

  • Tool Holders & Accessories

    The range of Wenza tooling is wide enough and includes turning holders for external and internal machining with indexable inserts, face and end mill carriers with indexable inserts and also drill bodies with indexable inserts.

    The use of tools with indexable inserts allows operations for various cutting materials and geometries as well as saves financial resources due to their versatility.

  • Threading Tools

    Wenza line of threading tools contains rotating and turning threading tools for threading a wide range of metric and inch threads. The combination of modern cutting materials and hardening techniques enables our tools to machine materials of varying complexity.

  • Parting & Grooving Tools

    Used for parting and grooving on internal or external surfaces. This line of tools consists of holders and indexable carbide inserts.

  • Taps and Forming Taps

    Taps are used for tapping internal threads, forming taps are used for rolling threads without chipping.

    When selecting this kind of tools, you should pay attention to the types of tool materials used for taps: HSS, HSSE, HSSE-PM

  • Exchangeable Head Drills

    Exchangeable head drills make the production process more versatile, without losing any machining speed compared to indexable insert drills.

  • Anti-Vibration Boring Bars

    Designed for deep hole boring and profiling with high productivity along with excellent surface finish. Machining with an overhang of 6 to 10 chuck diameters is possible.

  • Spindle Toolings

    Includes chucks, collets and accessories for fastening cutting tools and workpieces. This kind of tool is used for all types of machining.
